Description

Homemade lasagna recipe with easy steps to follow

Ingredients

  • Lasagna
  • Mozzarella
  • Egg
  • Beef and Sausage
  • Garlic
  • Pasta Sauce
  • Parsley
  • Onion
  • Tomato Paste
  • Parmesan
  • Spices

Steps

  1. Step 1: Boil pasta: Cook in a large pot of salted water.
  2. Step 2: Prepare meat sauce: Cook sausage and beef with onion and garlic. Drain well, add the pasta sauce & simmer it for a few minutes to thicken.
  3. Step 3: Combine cheese mixture: Stir the cheese mixture together in a bowl
  4. Step 4: Layer and bake:Layer the meat sauce and cheese mixture with lasagna noodles and bake until browned and bubbly.
